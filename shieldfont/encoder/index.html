§ 01HOW TO USECONTROLS EXPLAINED

Three things to know.

01

The four controls

Variant picks which mapping family is used. Size, line-height, and letter-spacing apply live to both the editor and the embed snippet — what you see is what you ship.

02

TEXT vs HTML view

TEXT shows the encoded prose with each substituted word in a gradient pill so you can see what changed. HTML shows the full embed snippet ready to paste.

03

Copy text vs Copy embed

Copy text gives you the raw encoded sentence. Copy embed gives you the link to the stylesheet plus the wrapped paragraph — paste it into any HTML page or custom HTML block.

Now ship it. Three ways.

Documents, articles, sites — the encoder output is the same. Pick the install route that matches where your content lives.
